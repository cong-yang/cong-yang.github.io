Cong Yang received the Ph.D. degree in computer
vision and pattern recognition from the University
of Siegen, Germany, in 2016. He was a Postdoctoral
Researcher at the MAGRIT Team, INRIA, France.
He worked scientifically and led the Computer
Vision and Machine Learning Teams at Clobotics
and Nanjing Horizon Robotics Technology Company
Ltd. He has been an Associate Professor with
Soochow University, since 2022. His main research
interests include computer vision, pattern recognition,
and their interdisciplinary applications.
